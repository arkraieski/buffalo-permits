# Buffalo Public-Domain Photo Assets

These files were downloaded from Library of Congress records selected for possible use on this site.

Use note:
- These are web-ready JPEG derivatives downloaded from LOC, not the largest available master files.
- If we want higher-resolution production assets later, use the LOC item pages below and pull the larger JPEG or TIFF versions.

## Design fit notes

- The two 1943 grain-boat photos are strong if we want Buffalo's industrial and canal identity to feel historic and civic.
- The two 2018 aerials are stronger for hero sections, banners, and modern geographic context.
- For homepage use, the downtown aerial is the most versatile and the Central Terminal aerial is the most distinctive.
